О проекте

Pangram Pangram is a Quebec foundry founded by designer Mathieu Desjardins. For the launch of the new "Editorial New" typeface, Pangram Pangram and Locomotive wanted to collaborate in order to create a microsite presenting the typography in action and pushing towards the online purchase

Задача

Our target audience being designers, art directors, developers and students, a very graphic approach was taken to showcase the beautiful set of variable fonts. The creative approach revolves around the typography itself, its vintage / modern duality and its creative abilities.
